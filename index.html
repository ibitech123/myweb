What is NAPLAN

NAPLAN (National Assessment Program â€“ Literacy and Numeracy) is a nationwide test for Australian students in Years 3, 5, 7, and 9. It assesses skills in four key areas: Reading, Writing, Language Conventions (spelling, grammar, punctuation), and Numeracy. NAPLAN helps track student progress and identifies areas for improvement.
